About BISMAC

Business Machine Corporation (BISMAC) is one of the largest integrated office machines and equipment distributors in the Philippines. The company began operating in 1954 as a department of Claude Wilson Trading and was officially incorporated on July 25, 1957.

BISMAC prides itself on having highly trained technicians and maintaining a large inventory of spare parts. Customers can choose from a wide range of office machines, from simple stand-alone units to full network systems.

Today, BISMAC positions itself as a total solutions provider. We provide the right technology and services so our clients can focus on growing their businesses.
